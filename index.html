InCySe Hackathon

Industrial Cybersecurity in a Connected World

Register Now

InCySe Objectives

Encourage students, especially from underrepresented groups, to consider a career in industrial cybersecurity. Impart knowledge of emerging technologies through hands-on learning facilitated by hackathon challenges.

Hackathon Overview

Duration: 1 week

Mode: Hybrid (Online and Onsite)

Participants: Teams of up to 2 students, mixing undergraduate and high school students.

Goals:

  • Introduce cybersecurity concepts and blockchain technology.
  • Foster problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation.
  • Develop practical prototypes addressing real-world challenges.

Timeline

March 21, 2025: Registration Opens
April 2, 2025: Learning and Ideation
April 4, 2025: Tutorials and Workshops
April 11, 2025: Demonstration and Final Presentation

Registration Opens

Participants can register online through the official hackathon website. Ensure all team members are registered by the deadline to secure a spot.

Learning and Ideation

  • Keynote & Inspiration - 1 Hour: "Securing the Future: Blockchain’s Role in Cybersecurity."
  • Workshops - 3 Hours: Cybersecurity Basics, Blockchain Fundamentals, Tech Tools.
  • Challenge Announcement - 30 Minutes: Reveal hackathon themes and problem statements.

Tutorials and Workshops

  • Blockchain Technology Overview
  • Cybersecurity Tools Demonstration
  • Practical Application of Python in Cybersecurity

Workshops will be led by industry experts and will include interactive activities.

Demonstration and Final Presentation

  • Development Phase - 6 Hours: Teams build prototypes with mentor support.
  • Presentation and Judging - 3 Hours: Demo and Q&A per team.
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Innovation - 20%
    • Technical Feasibility - 30%
    • Real-World Impact - 20%
    • Presentation - 10%
    • Teamwork - 20%
